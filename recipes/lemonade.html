Lemonade Recipe

a beverage of sweetened lemon juice mixed with water

Everyday becomes sweet when I drink lemonade

ingredients:

  • Lemon juice
  • sugar
  • Lemon slices
  • Water
  1. Juice your lemon
  2. Make a simple syrup by combining water and sugar in a small saucepan
  3. Stir all the ingredients
  4. Finally, enjoy your drink!
Sweet dreams are made of lemonade.
