Pinkpasta Recipe

Whenever you are hungry and need something easy to make! always go for pinkpasta :)

Pinkpasta is one of the most delicious food you would try!

Don't worry!it is very easy let's talk about the ingredients we need.

ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Chicken breast
  • Tomato paste
  • Cooking cream
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Make the pasta
  2. Heat butter over medium heat until it melts
  3. Next, add the diced onion and garlic until softened
  4. after that, heat small spoon of butter 'if it's needed' to cook the chicken
  5. Add the tomato paste with all the spices "onion powder, paprika, etc..
  6. add the cooking cream let it heat for two 5 minutes
  7. Last step, add the cooked pasta back into the skillet and stir until the pasta is evenly coated in the sauce.

    8. That's it! enjoy the delicious meal.
